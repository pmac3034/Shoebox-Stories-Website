Simple Process

How It Works

Three easy steps stand between your shoebox and a lifetime of digital memories.

Step 01
📬

Ship Your Photos

Pack up your photos, slides, negatives, or tapes and send them to us using your own shipping or our prepaid label. No drop-off required.

Step 02
🔬

We Scan & Enhance

Our team professionally scans every item at high resolution, then applies color correction, AI sharpening, and dust removal. A human reviews every scan.

Step 03
🎉

Get Your Memories

You receive a secure private download link with your digital files organized into folders. Your originals are returned to you, insured and tracked.

What We Scan

Prints & Photos

Color, B&W, all eras

Prints up to 8.5×11" — from modern snapshots to century-old portraits — scanned at 600 DPI with full color correction. Back-of-photo notes captured at no extra charge.

600 DPI Color & B&W Up to 8.5×11"

Slides

Mounted & loose, all formats

Standard 2×2" mounted slides in 110, 126, 127, half-135, 135 (35mm), and 828 formats. Mounted or loose, scanned at 4,000 DPI for exceptional detail.

4,000 DPI 35mm & More Mounted or Loose
🎬

Negatives

Strip or cut, all sizes

Film negatives in 35mm, 110, APS, 135, 126, 127, and 120 formats — strip or cut — scanned at 4,000 DPI and delivered as true-color positive images.

4,000 DPI 35mm & 120 Strip or Cut

Film & Video

Reels & tapes to MP4

Super 8, 8mm, and 16mm film reels, plus VHS, Hi8, Digital8, and MiniDV tapes — all transferred to HD digital MP4 files you can watch and share anywhere.
